SoCal fires evacuation map

The map below shows the current evacuation zones for fires in the Los Angeles area. Use the buttons to zoom in or out.

Evacuation orders are lawful orders to leave because of an immediate threat to life. These areas are lawfully closed to the public.

Evacuation warnings are notices of a potential threat to life/property. Those who require additional time to evacuate should leave now.
